About Me

I am Mohammad Al-Hajjeeh, a Software Engineering graduate from the Jordan University of Science and Technology with a GPA of 3.18 (Very Good). Additionally, I bring a full year of enterprise experience as an Oracle Technical Consultant at Macquiers Company. During this tenure, I mastered Oracle Fusion SaaS, specializing in developing custom and standard BI Publisher reports for Financial, Supply Chain, and HR modules. My expertise also includes Oracle Fusion Personalization using Sandbox, Page Composer, and custom screen development using Visual Builder.
